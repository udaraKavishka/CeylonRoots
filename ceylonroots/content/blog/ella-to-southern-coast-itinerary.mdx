If you want both mountain scenery and beach time, the Ella-to-south route is one of Sri Lanka's best combinations.

## Day-by-Day Breakdown

### Day 1: Arrive in Ella

Settle in and visit **Nine Arches Bridge** around sunset. Keep dinner local and rest early.

### Day 2: Little Adam's Peak and Tea Stops

Do a sunrise hike to **Little Adam's Peak**, then visit a tea estate before heading south.

### Day 3: Udawalawe Stop

On the way to the coast, include a half-day safari in **Udawalawe National Park**.

### Day 4: Mirissa and Whale-Watching Season

Enjoy beach time and optional whale watching (best between November and April).

### Day 5: Galle Fort Walk

Spend your final day in **Galle Fort** with cafe stops, local boutiques, and ocean-front sunset points.

## Budget Guidance

- Mid-range private transfer per day: generally predictable and efficient.
- Boutique stays in Ella and Mirissa book quickly in high season.
- Reserve safari slots one day ahead.

This route is ideal for travelers who want photography, light adventure, and downtime in one compact itinerary.
