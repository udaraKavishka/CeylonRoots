Sri Lanka is one of Asia's top wildlife destinations for compact yet diverse safari experiences.

## Park Selection by Interest

- **Yala**: leopard probability and varied terrain.
- **Udawalawe**: elephant sightings and family-friendly access.
- **Wilpattu**: quieter routes and longer tracking drives.

## Best Time and Timing

- Dry months usually improve visibility and road conditions.
- Morning drives offer cooler weather and active wildlife.
- Evening drives are strong for atmosphere and photography.

## Safari Booking Tips

- Use licensed guides and trusted jeep operators.
- Avoid overpacked routes during peak holidays.
- Carry hats, neutral clothing, and sun protection.

## Responsible Wildlife Etiquette

- Keep distance and avoid loud behavior.
- Do not request off-track driving near animals.
- Support operators who follow park rules strictly.

Pairing one safari with a cultural or beach segment usually creates the best overall Sri Lanka itinerary.
