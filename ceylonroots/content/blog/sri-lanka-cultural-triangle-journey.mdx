Sri Lanka's Cultural Triangle is one of the best places to begin your journey. It gives you history, sacred sites, local village life, and some of the most iconic views in the country.

## Suggested 4-Day Route

### Day 1: Dambulla and Sigiriya

Start with the **Dambulla Cave Temple** in the morning, then continue to **Sigiriya Rock Fortress** by late afternoon for cooler weather and golden-hour views.

### Day 2: Polonnaruwa and Minneriya

Visit the ancient city of **Polonnaruwa** early. In the evening, head to **Minneriya National Park** for elephant gatherings (seasonal).

### Day 3: Village and Spice Experience

Take a slower day with a village cooking session and a spice-garden stop on your way to Kandy.

### Day 4: Kandy Heritage Highlights

Visit the **Temple of the Tooth Relic**, local markets, and a Kandyan cultural dance performance.

## Practical Tips

- Wear light, breathable clothes and carry water.
- Start major climbs early in the day.
- Keep shoulders and knees covered at sacred sites.
- Book guides in advance during peak season.

## Why This Route Works

It balances archaeological depth with nature and local experiences, without feeling rushed. Travelers can understand the island's heritage while still keeping comfort and pacing in mind.
